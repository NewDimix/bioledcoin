Ledbiocoin Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorist Financing Policies

Ledbiocoin undertakes to comply with anti-money laundering (hereinafter AML Policy) and the counter-terrorist financing laws (AML / CTF).

All members of the Ledbiocoin team, Ledbiocoin departments, and structural units, without exception, must be governed by the AML / CTF Policy, including new and existing employees, who must be instructed accordingly.

In order to help government agencies combat money laundering and terrorist financing, all financial institutions are required to collect, verify and store data identifying the account holder. And for these purposes, the Ledbiocoin team has developed an internal anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing policies.

Ledbiocoin’s AML policy is based on the degree of risks analysis and is designed to solve the following tasks:

  • the exclusion of direct or indirect complicity, when using Ledbiocoin services, for the performance of actions contrary to the current legislation and legal norms;
  • the counteraction and identification of any attempts and forms of money laundering and terrorist financing;
  • providing a mode of operation that excludes any transfer and receipt of funds without proper clearance by Ledbiocoin and Users;
  • the maintaining and strengthening the reputation of Ledbiocoin at the international level by applying (in case of necessity) the recommendations of the Financial Action Task Force (FATF).

When registering for the Ledbiocoin project, the user assumes the following obligations:

  • the user undertakes to comply with all applicable laws on anti-money laundering, including but not limited to AML / CTF Policy, and follow all associated rules and instructions (if necessary);
  • the user confirms that he has no information or suspicions regarding the fact that the funds used to replenish the account in the past, present or future are obtained from an illegal source or have any relation to money laundering or other unlawful activities banned by the current legislation or instructions of any international organizations;
  • the user confirms that he has no information or suspicions about the fact that the profit received from investments will be used to finance illegal activities;
  • the user agrees to immediately provide any information that Ledbiocoin deems necessary to request for compliance with the draft legislation and regulatory requirements for anti-money laundering.

In accordance with the AML Policy, Ledbiocoin will conduct initial and ongoing User of the project identity checks as part of the “Know Your Customer” (KYC) mandatory procedure in accordance with the level of potential risk associated with each User.

Ledbiocoin has the right to require from each user of the project minimal information that confirms the identity of the client. Ledbiocoin will store this information with the methods and results of the check. Ledbiocoin will inform the user about the detection of any inconsistencies or suspicions when identifying him. The minimum set of the User’s identification data includes:

  • full name;
  • date of birth;
  • citizenship;
  • the address of permanent residence;
  • e-mail address.

To confirm and verify the authenticity of the above data, Ledbiocoin may require the User to provide the following information:

  • valid passport;
  • national identity card (driver’s license, military ID, etc.);
  • a document confirming the address of permanent residence at a given time (bill for payment for utility services, a statement on the status of the bank account, etc.)

To minimize the risks associated with money laundering and the financing of terrorist activities, Ledbiocoin does not accept cash funds from the Users in the project under any circumstances and, accordingly, does not pay out cash. Ledbiocoin reserves the right to refuse to process the transaction at any stage in the event that the transaction is in any way related to money laundering or criminal activity.

Ledbiocoin does not conduct operations with residents of countries that are recognized by international supervisory authorities, including the Financial Action Task Force (FATF), as those that do not meet the requirements of the current legislation in terms of anti-money laundering and do not adequately combat the financing of terrorism. Ledbiocoin does not register such customers and does not accept funds from them.

Ledbiocoin also has the right to notify the relevant state and other interested authorities about detected cases of violation by the User of domestic anti-money laundering and the counter-terrorist financing policies.
